This website houses Cooky, an AI that will generate a made up recipe based on a series of recipes. These recipes are supposed to be fun! If you try any of the recipes, use #AIRecipeBot on social media.

This project was completed as part of the HobbyHacks 2020 Virtual Hackathon hosted by TechTogether. It was a collaboration between Sornali Banik, Salwa Din, Mona Kalaoun, Kaelin Panneton, and Muchen Zhang.
